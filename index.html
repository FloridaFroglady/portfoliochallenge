About Me

As you can tell from the photos, I have two lazy roomies and live in beautiful Tampa Bay, FL.

I've been puttering about with computers since my Tandy CoCo with its cassette tape drive.
I taught myself HTML in the early 90's and made webPAGES for people on Geocities, Angelfire, MySpace and several PBBGs.
I also made some small business webPAGES for friends and relatives.

It was all for fun, but I didn't have time to go back to school and get a degree in it.
Then I retired from nuclear medicine and had the opportunity to get some formal training. Now I make webAPPS.
